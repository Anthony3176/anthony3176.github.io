Combating Hate Speech

The Statistics

Hate speech and harrassment is a growing problem in this modern world.

    According to ADL,
  • "41% of respondents who experienced a physical threat stated that the platform took no action on a threatening post, an increase from the 38% who had reported a similar lack of action the year before."
  • "African-American respondents reported a sharp rise in race-based harassment, from 42% last year to this year."

No matter who you are or what your background is, these numbers should be disturbing.

Getting Help

If you or a person you know is experiencing any form of hate speech, here are things that can help you,

  1. kidshelpphone.ca helps you through the phone, either by text message or a phone call.
  2. Block the harrasser.
  3. Report the harrasser.
  4. In some instances, there are moderators of the community you can report the harrasser to.
  5. Sometimes, the harrasser is only looking for attention or a reaction from you. If you feel like this is the case, ignore the harrasser. If they do not stop, immedietly use any of the resources above.

If you experience a hate crime, please go to the police or call 911.
